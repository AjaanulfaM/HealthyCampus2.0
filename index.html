About Us



Who are we?


We are an initiative based at DkIT that aspires to promote a culture that enhances the health and wellbeing of the DkIT college community through a holistic whole of campus approach that enables our staff and students to achieve their full potential using the Healthy Campus Framework.


The responsibilities of the Healthy Campus Initiative are:



Promoting physical well-being


Fostering mental health and emotional well-being


Promoting healthy eating habits


Creating a tobacco-free and substance-free environment


Promoting overall well-being and work-life balance


Our Goals


Given that Ireland is ranked as the 41st most obese country globally, with an obesity rate of approximately 25.3%, our health-based initiative recognizes the critical importance of addressing this issue. Our goal is not only to ensure that our students successfully graduate from Dundalk Institute of Technology (DkIT) but also to promote their long-term well-being. We are committed to fostering a healthy, happy, and balanced lifestyle for our students during their time at DkIT and beyond. By providing comprehensive support and resources, we aim to equip our students with the knowledge and habits necessary to maintain their health throughout their lives.

To achieve this, our initiative offers a range of programs and services designed to support physical, mental, and emotional well-being. We provide educational workshops on nutrition, exercise, and stress management, alongside access to fitness facilities and mental health resources. Additionally, we organize community events and activities that encourage healthy living and foster a sense of belonging. By creating an environment that prioritizes wellness, we strive to empower our students to make informed health choices, ultimately leading to a more vibrant and resilient campus community.
